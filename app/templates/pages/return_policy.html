Return Policy

We want you to be happy with every order. If something isn't right, here's how returns work at {{ app_name }}.

Eligibility
  • Returns must be requested within 24 hours of delivery for perishable items (fruits, vegetables, dairy).
  • Non-perishable items can be returned within 7 days of delivery, unopened and in original packaging.
  • Items must not be used, damaged, or missing parts.
How to Request a Return
  1. Go to My Orders.
  2. Select the order and item you want to return.
  3. Choose a reason and submit the request.
Refunds

Once your return is approved, refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Questions? Contact us anytime.
